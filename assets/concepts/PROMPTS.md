# Ticker concept-art prompts — run each, drop ALL outputs in this folder
Any filenames fine (untitled ok). 1–2 takes per prompt is plenty. Claude sorts + picks.
Shared rules baked into every prompt: bold simple shapes, thick outlines, flat colors,
front-facing full body, centered, plain white background, no text/watermark — this is
what survives shrinking to 64 pixels.

## 1 — The Worrier (baseline)
Cute cartoon mascot character: a round anxious heart with a worried face, big expressive
eyes, tiny stubby feet, wearing a small football scarf. Thick dark outlines, flat colors,
no gradients, simple bold shapes like a 90s video game mascot. Front-facing, full body,
centered, neutral standing pose, plain white background, no text.

## 2 — The Squish (blob energy)
Cute squishy blob-shaped heart creature, soft rounded body like a stress ball, huge
nervous glossy eyes, tiny nub feet, small football scarf around its middle. Chunky
cartoon style, thick outlines, flat colors, no gradients, minimal detail, adorable and
slightly panicked. Front-facing, full body, centered, plain white background, no text.

## 3 — The Scrapper (determined underdog)
Small tough cartoon heart mascot with a determined scrunched face, furrowed brows, little
clenched fists, band-aid on one cheek, football scarf tied like a headband. Bold retro
game mascot style, thick outlines, flat colors, simple shapes, front-facing, full body,
centered, plain white background, no text.

## 4 — The Softie (maximum sympathy)
Heartbreakingly cute round heart creature with enormous watery puppy eyes, tiny worried
mouth, small stubby arms hugging itself, little football scarf. Soft kawaii mascot style
but with thick clean outlines and flat colors, no gradients, very simple shapes,
front-facing, full body, centered, plain white background, no text.

## 5 — The Retro Japanese Mascot (Kirby energy)
Simple round heart character in classic Japanese video game mascot style, dot eyes with
big white highlights, wide expressive mouth, rosy cheeks, stubby feet, tiny football
scarf. Designed like a 1998 Game Boy game hero, extremely simple bold shapes, thick
outlines, flat colors, front-facing, full body, centered, plain white background, no text.

## 6 — The Cardiac Patient (leaning into the bit)
Cute nervous heart mascot that looks like it just survived a scare: slightly sweating,
one drop on forehead, wide alert eyes, holding one tiny hand on its chest, small football
scarf, faint stitch line down its side like an old scar. Bold cartoon style, thick
outlines, flat colors, simple shapes, front-facing, full body, centered, plain white
background, no text.
